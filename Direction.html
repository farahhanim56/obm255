Public Transport Suggestion

Option 1: Train + Taxi/Grab

  • If you are starting from Kuala Lumpur or other major cities, you can take the ETS train to Sungai Petani. Check the schedule on the KTMB website or app for times.
  • Upon arrival at Sungai Petani Railway Station, you can take a taxi or use a ride-hailing service such as Grab to get to The Jerai Hotel. The hotel is located about 20-25 minutes from the train station by car.

Option 2: Bus + Taxi/Grab

  • If you choose to take a bus, several express bus services operate from major cities. So, you can check the bus schedule online for a trip to Sungai Petani.
  • Upon arriving at Sungai Petani Bus Terminal, you can take a taxi or Grab to The Jerai Hotel, which is approximately 10-15 minutes away from the terminal.

Option 3: Share a ride

  • If you have access to a car or prefer a ride-sharing service like GrabCar, you can drive directly to The Jerai Hotel. With the help of navigation apps like Google Maps or Waze for the best route based on current traffic conditions.

Other Information You May Be Concerned About:

  • Ensure to check the train or bus schedules well in advance to plan your journey accordingly, especially for early morning departures.
  • Please download the Grab application if you plan to use the ride-hailing service in Sungai Petani.
  • Double check your reservation at The Jerai Hotel and inform your expected arrival time if you booked a room to rest.
